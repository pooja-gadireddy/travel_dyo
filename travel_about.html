Hello, World! My name is Pooja.

This is a webpage all about my love for travelling, including where I have traveled so far and where I hope to visit some day.

Introduction

I love to learn more about the cultures, food, and traditions around me - near and far. I have always been a mountain over beach person. The mountains have this beautiful smell in the air and I find it very calming. Plus, the sunrises and sunsets in the mountains are unbeatable.

I haven't travelled outside of North Carolina much but I have big plans for the future. I would love to visit at least two countries in each continent, learn about the daily life and traditions of the locals, and dive into and explore various ethnic cuisines.
